Texas Child-Centered Care · DFPS Credentialing Support

ComplianceReady is T3C Ready — because your software should be too.

Texas Child-Centered Care (T3C) represents a complete transformation of the foster care system — replacing the Service Level System with 24 clearly defined Service Packages, new credentialing requirements, and higher documentation standards for every GRO and RTC in Texas.

All residential childcare providers must transition to T3C by August 2027. ComplianceReady was designed with this transition in mind — helping your facility stay fully documented, staff properly trained, and credentials supported every step of the way.
